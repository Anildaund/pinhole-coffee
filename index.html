Pillars of Pinhole

We are a Women-owned, Asian-owned, and LGBTQ+ owned heartbeat. Our whimsical, cozy space is designed to be the "light source" for the community.

140 Years in the Making

Built in the 1880s, our corner was originally Max Breithaupt's butcher shop. We trade in heritage, preserving the local soul while brewing the city's finest espresso.
